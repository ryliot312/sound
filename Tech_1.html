Essential Expereience

You walk into a white room with only bluetooth headphones on. One Azure in the front of the room. It’s mostly silent. You’re a little confused, and that confusion amplifies when you hear a strange-pitched noise in one of the corners of the room. You walk over to investigate, noticing that the sound gets louder as you walk near. Once you feel as if you’ve found where the source is coming from, a grand soundscape overtakes you and the room, almost as if the air around you has changed. Now, you hear a barrage of sounds, coming from all different places in the room. You freely investigate each sound, noticing that as you walk near a certain sound source, it becomes louder and the focus of your hearing. You walk to one side of the room, noticing that the reverb and ambience changes, almost as if you are walking into a building. Now the room feels like a giant cathedral, with massive echoes and chimes in the distance. You walk back the way you came and notice that it sounds as if you exited the building. You keep exploring all sides of the room, finding new and exciting sounds to discover.
